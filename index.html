Visit Rancho Cucamonga

Located near the San Gabriel Mountains, this city may seem small but there are still ways to have fun!

Top three activities to do at Rancho

Enjoy Red Hill Park

This park is friendly for all ages. There's even a pond to sit by and look at, but don't feed the ducks and turtles.

Explore Victoria Gardens Mall

Whether you want to shop at high end stores, go out to eat or just walk around and enjoy the open-air atmosphere in this outdoor mall with lots of things to do with your loved ones, or even yourself.

Attend an escape room

If you want a fun challenge for you and your friends, you can try to solve this 60 minute challenge.
